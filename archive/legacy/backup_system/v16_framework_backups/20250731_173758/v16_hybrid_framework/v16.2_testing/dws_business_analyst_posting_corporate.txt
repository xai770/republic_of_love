Senior Financial Analyst - Corporate Development Division
Deutsche Industriebank AG
Frankfurt am Main, Germany

Position Summary:
Deutsche Industriebank AG seeks a highly qualified Senior Financial Analyst to join our Corporate Development Division. The successful candidate will be responsible for complex financial analysis, strategic planning, and cross-functional collaboration within our investment banking operations.

Key Responsibilities:
• Conduct comprehensive financial modeling and valuation analysis for M&A transactions
• Prepare detailed investment memoranda and presentations for executive leadership
• Collaborate with internal stakeholders including Risk Management, Compliance, and Operations
• Perform due diligence analysis on potential acquisition targets and investment opportunities
• Monitor and analyze market trends, competitor performance, and regulatory developments
• Support quarterly earnings processes and investor relations activities
• Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and internal risk management policies
• Mentor junior analysts and coordinate with external advisors and consultants

Required Qualifications:
• Master's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field from accredited institution
• Minimum 5 years of experience in investment banking, corporate finance, or related field
• Advanced proficiency in financial modeling using Excel and Bloomberg Terminal
• Experience with valuation methodologies including DCF, comparable company analysis, and LBO modeling
• Strong knowledge of German and European financial regulations (MiFID II, Basel III)
• Professional certification (CFA, FRM) preferred but not required
• Exceptional analytical and quantitative skills with attention to detail
• Fluent in German and English (written and verbal); additional European languages advantageous

Technical Requirements:
• Advanced Excel skills including VBA programming
• Proficiency with Bloomberg, FactSet, or similar financial data platforms
• Experience with SQL databases and data analysis tools
• Knowledge of PowerPoint for executive-level presentations
• Familiarity with project management software (MS Project, Asana)

Personal Attributes:
• Strong leadership and team collaboration skills
• Ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills
• High ethical standards and integrity
• Adaptability and continuous learning mindset

Compensation & Benefits:
• Competitive base salary commensurate with experience (€85,000 - €120,000)
• Performance-based bonus structure (target 20-40% of base salary)
• Comprehensive health insurance and pension contributions
• 30 days annual vacation plus German public holidays
• Professional development budget and continuing education support
• Flexible working arrangements including hybrid home office options
