## Business Analyst (E-invoicing) - Requirements & Responsibilities

### Your Tasks
* **Process Management:** Develop and implement e-invoicing workflows including BAU processes and system integration
* **Data Verification:** Verify invoice information accuracy and ensure compliance with legal frameworks and contracts  
* **Financial Monitoring:** Monitor fee transactions, ensure timely payments, and report overdue invoices
* **Issue Resolution:** Analyze and resolve billing discrepancies and cash breaks through systematic investigation
* **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Collaborate with internal teams (Finance, IT) and external partners for seamless operations
* **Process Improvement:** Support continuous improvement initiatives and document standardized procedures

### Your Profile  
* **Education & Experience:** Economics degree or equivalent; experience in asset management operations preferred
* **Technical Skills:** Proficiency in SimCorp Dimension, SAP R3/SAP4Hana, Excel, and MS Office applications
* **Language Skills:** Fluent German and English (written and spoken); French advantageous
* **Other:** Strong analytical and communication skills, team-oriented, proactive problem-solving approach
