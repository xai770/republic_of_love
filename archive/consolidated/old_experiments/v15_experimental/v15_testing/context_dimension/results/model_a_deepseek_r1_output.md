## Your Tasks

* Prepare and deliver standardized reports for finance departments using appropriate tools.
* Engage proactively with internal/external stakeholders across various business units during daily operational support.
* Evaluate new contract requirements systematically to assess their impact on the E-invoicing process.
* Define scope, objectives, and timelines for change initiatives and improvement projects within established guidelines.
* Document existing processes comprehensively and develop standardized procedures/methodologies (incl. KPIs/KRIs).
* Implement business strategies and goals across relevant product/business units while ensuring adherence to all internal/external regulations.

* Education & Experience: Bachelor's degree preferably in economics or a related field; experience within Asset Management operations, finance compliance, or administration is required.
* Technical Skills: Proficiency with the DWS core operational system (SimCorp Dimension); knowledge of other relevant systems like SAP R3/R4Hana and Aladdin/Algo preferred. Ability to interact effectively with diverse IT systems.
* Language Skills: Excellent command of German and English (both written and spoken) at a business fluency level; basic understanding of French is advantageous.
* Other categories as relevant:
  * Familiarity with fundamental bookkeeping principles, especially FODS/financial accounting concepts.
  * Experience working directly or indirectly with internal/external financial customers/service providers essential.
  * Proven collaborative skills and ability to share expertise effectively within a team environment.
