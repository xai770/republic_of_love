Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this website is talent.yoga. For contact details, see our Impressum.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data:

  • Account Data: Email address and name (via Google OAuth)
  • Profile Data: Work history, skills, job preferences you provide
  • Usage Data: Which job matches you view, rate, or mark as applied

We do not collect:

  • Your CV files (we extract data, then discard the file)
  • Location tracking or device fingerprinting
  • Third-party analytics or advertising cookies

3. Why We Process Your Data

We process your data to:

  • Match your skills to job postings (legitimate interest)
  • Show you personalized job recommendations
  • Improve our matching algorithms
  • Communicate service updates (only in-app, no email)

4. How We Store Your Data

  • Location: All data is stored on servers in Germany
  • Encryption: Data encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Retention: Data kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all data
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing for legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@talent.yoga

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: For authentication only. We receive your email and name. Google Privacy Policy
  • Hetzner: Server hosting in Germany. Hetzner Privacy Policy

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies:

  • Session cookie: Keeps you logged in (expires on browser close)

No tracking cookies. No analytics cookies. No third-party cookies.

8. Data Processing

Your skills and work history are processed by local AI models (running on our servers, not sent to external AI services). No data is sent to OpenAI, Google AI, or similar third-party AI providers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notification.
