# Our commitment to you

talent.yoga does not charge for hope.

It charges for intelligence, clarity, and support.

This is who we are and what we commit to. It is not a terms-of-service document. It is not marketing. It is a description of how we intend to treat you — and why.

---

## What we are

A job-search companion. Not a job board. Not a recruitment agency. Not a platform that profits from your anxiety.

We match you to real job postings, tell you truthfully why a match looks good and why it might not, and give you intelligence you cannot get anywhere else — employer history, posting patterns, news signals. Everything we build is shaped by one constraint: your dignity.

---

## What we offer

### Free membership — €0

No credit card. No expiry. No tricks.

- 10 job matches per month — enough to act on, not enough to overwhelm
- Mira, our AI companion, available for 5 conversations per day
- Your full profile and job-search journey, tracked and yours

Free is not a trial. It is a real membership for people who want a careful, low-volume search, or who are not ready to commit yet. It is free because we believe people in difficult situations should not be priced out of help.

---

### Standard membership — €5 / month

The main membership. Most people who are actively searching should be here.

- Unlimited job matching
- Mira, fully unlocked — CV feedback, interview preparation, application review, honest assessment of your chances (no empty encouragement)
- Full employer rap sheet — founding details, posting history, recent news, community reviews
- Full dashboard — posting trends, geographic breakdown, personal match history

€5 is the cost of one coffee. The employer intelligence alone is worth more than that.

You can pause your membership at any time without penalty. Job searches have phases. We do not charge you for quiet months.

---

### Sustainer membership — €10+ / month

Everything in Standard, plus a deliberate choice.

If you can afford more, you can fund a free membership for someone who cannot. The minimum is €10. There is no maximum.

Sustainers are listed by name or alias on our public supporters page. That is the only difference. Sustainer is not a premium tier. It is an act of solidarity — and the mechanism that makes this whole system possible.

---

## What we will never do

- Guarantee outcomes. We will tell you the truth about your chances. That is more valuable than false hope.
- Sell your data to anyone, for any reason, ever.
- Show your email to anyone, including ourselves. It is encrypted and masked at the point of entry.
- Use your desperation against you. No countdown timers, no scarcity tricks, no dark patterns.
- Profit from your anxiety. We are not designed to keep you searching longer than necessary.

---

## How the money works

We show you exactly what your membership costs us to provide. Not an approximation — actual compute, tooling, and matching costs, visible under your account.

Standard at €5 is below cost for heavy users. Above cost for light ones. In aggregate it sustains the platform. Sustainers at €10+ cover free members who cannot pay.

The founder of this platform is currently in personal debt to keep it running. That debt is published, not hidden. When the platform becomes a non-profit, that structure formalises what is already the practice: every euro goes to keeping the service running and accessible.

You are not a revenue line. You are the reason the platform exists.

---

## How we prioritise when the system is under load

If our servers are busy, we queue requests fairly:

1. Sustainers are served first — they make the free tier possible
2. Standard members second
3. Free members third

Within each group: members who have used less AI capacity this period are served before those who have used more. If you use Mira sparingly, your next request moves up the queue. We think that is fair.

## The journey

Job searching on talent.yoga is named. Not gamified — named.

Every real thing that happens to you in a job search has a status: *Novice Yogi*, *Matched Yogi*, *Resilient Yogi*, *Patient Yogi*, *Victorious Yogi*. These names exist because job searching is genuinely hard and the steps deserve to be acknowledged. You are not stuck. You are somewhere on a path.

---

## After you find work

We hope you leave. That is what success looks like here.

When you come back — to hire, to mentor, to sustain the platform for the next person — you will be welcome. Some of our most important members are people who found work here and returned to help others do the same.
